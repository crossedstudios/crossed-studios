My name is Ray Tam

My name is Ray Tam. I create visual work that sits between observation and intention.

Photography, video, and motion are my ways of slowing down to pay closer attention.

Crossed Studios is a collection of these studies, fragments, and moments I didn’t want to lose.

Disciplines

01 / Photography

Capturing stillness, texture, and the quiet spaces in between. Specialized in portraits, street documentation, and low-light environments that feel observed, not staged.

02 / Motion

Cutting for atmosphere, pacing, and presence. Creating short films, promotional narratives, and event clips driven by movement that feels intentional, never loud.
