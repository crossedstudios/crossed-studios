CROSSED STUDIOS

About

My name is Ray Tam

I create visual work that sits somewhere between observation and intention.

Photography, video, and motion are my ways of slowing things down and paying attention.

Crossed Studios is a collection of studies, fragments, and moments I didn’t want to lose.
